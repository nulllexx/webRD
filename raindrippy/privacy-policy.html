Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RainDrippy collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our games, websites, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an account, we store your username and your securely hashed password in our database. Although we collect DOB at signup, this is NOT stored anywhere -- it is processed and then immediately discarded.
  • Gameplay information: We may store progress, achievements, or preferences linked to your account.
  • Technical information: Basic system data (IP address, device identificator, browser) may be logged for security and troubleshooting.
  • Cookies: We only use essential cookies to keep you signed in and ensure game functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To save game progress and preferences.
  • To secure our systems and prevent fraud.
  • To provide support.

4. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies only. These are required for logging in and maintaining sessions. Without them, our services may not function properly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with service providers who assist us in operating our services, provided they agree to keep your information confidential. All of your data is stored securely on our servers & sensitive information is always encrypted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

For some of these rights, we have automated secure services at your disposal to exercise the respective rights: click here.

For the remaining rights, you can exercise them by mailing us at raindrippy.contact@protonmail.com

7. Data Retention

Account data is stored as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, associated personal data will be removed from our systems immediately. We may retain certain information as required by law.

8. Security

We take security very seriously. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 and are never stored in plain text. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at raindrippy.contact@protonmail.com.

10. Updates to this policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. You will be notified of these changes via a notice on our website upon accessing it. We also encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
